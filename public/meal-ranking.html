The Best Meals According to Brittany

My top 3 meals of all-time.

Chicken Spaghetti

Chicken spaghetti is a one dish recipe that is so easy and very delicious.

Chicken and Rice burritos

Chicken and rice burritos are the best meal prep meal. I can make a couple of weeks of burritos and freeze them. When we want them, reheat and add a sauce that you are feeling to change the flavor.

Steak and artichokes

M husband and I like to make steak and artichokes for our anniversary every year. It will always be a personal favorite!
